Grocery Case Study

Growing up I enjoyed cooking with my mom and the only way to buy foodstuff was to get it at the popular mile 12 market, which is always crowded. but with the popularity of the internet and mobile phones, purchasing grocery online has enabled all of us to have control over what we purchase online, include the type of Grocery.

MY GOAL:

My major focus was on the client's audience who are the customers and buyers. I kept in mind that I need to help new users understand what the product is all about, and help returning users find the information they need. My goal was to design an accessible application that is easy to use by different kind of users.

MY ROLE

Product Design, User Research, Wireframe

Sketching and Wireframe
